Manifold Matters

Welcome! My name is Barbera Brakel, and under the collective name Manifold Matters, I engage in a diverse range of activities within the realm of online research, (generative) artificial intelligence, and (privacy-enhancing) decentralized technologies. Fascinated by AI as a tool to enhance our cognitive abilities, I explore how it can sharpen problem-solving skills, ignite creativity, and transform knowledge management.

With a background spanning years in both the cultural sector and the intelligence community and law enforcement, I have built up a rich experience in researching with and into new technologies. From this broad perspective, I am able to create connections between culture, innovative technology, and privacy. I advise knowledge workers on how to navigate and work more efficiently with the most recent applications in the field of AI. Additionally, with this unique mix of knowledge and skills, I initiate my own projects.
